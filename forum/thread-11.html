(As per Duchess's request, this discussion is for planning what you all want to do on the 16th, after having rescued the captains.) You all land in a small fleet of longboats with the loyal-to-Montegue captains a ways north of the city, and secret yourselves in the woods. Those of you who feel you might be recognized change clothes again, and some head back into the town to see what's happening. Here's the situation as you arrive: -The mansion is almost done being packed up. Raymonde (the guy Sebastien chatted with) is just heading down to the docks, carrying a small chest, and surrounded by porters. Pansemne (the servant girl that can animal-speak) is standing in the doorway, watching them go. -The docks at Kem are alive with lights and activity. Every long-boat available is ferrying sailors and supplies out to the boats. -Peering out to sea with your spyglass, you see the main fleet, as expected, but also two boats breaking away to the north and another two breaking away to the south. Both are awash with lights, and bulls-eye lanterns playing over the coast. -Also, two ships are limping back to the docks. One has a couple holes blown into its starboard side, and the other has a gaping blackened hole in its port side. -Having been told that his orders are waiting for him at the tavern, Charles slips in and grabs them. They read:

You are charged with two tasks. Your first and primary duty is to ensure that Montegue dies. The easiest way would be to encourage him to pursue us in the ships we have left for him. After the explosion, be sure to finish the job. Your next task is to expedite the extraction of Vienne and Nicolette. They will meet you in St. Tremult in two days at the usual location. Your success will bring you the gratitude of L'Empereur. Should you fail, I recommend a visit to Molhyna, as it is the one place on Theah the furthest from his reach. Although I doubt you could escape his clutches even in the mythical New World the Vesten are going on about. Good luck. -Toussaint
-It is perhaps 3:00 in the morning. What do you, the protagonists, do next?
