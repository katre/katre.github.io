I've put together a cleaned-up version of this at: http://spod-central.org/~lpsmith/rpg/transcripts/conan/SpiritOfConan_rmap.jpg Only one crossed line! If anyone cares about continuity, here's the current timeline, based on people's introduction stories, and some of my own character write-up. These four go in this order at present, though the second and third could be switched with a slight tweak to my write-up: The Slaughter in the Fane: Introduces Sylith and Iason; possible introduction of Everard Lojban the Destroyer vs. the Demon-Beast of Yucca Flats: Sylith and Everard's story continue; introduction of Lojban Iason vs. the Volcano God: Iason and Lojban's stories continue; possible introduction of Joden Cain and the Ashen Curse: Sylith and Iason's stories continue; Cain introduced The next two stories come after the first four, in either order: Tack and the Tomb of the Priests: Cain and Lojban's stories continue; possible introduction of Tack Joden goes to Hell: Cain and Joden's stories continue; possible introduction of Tack And the last story can take pace any time in the continuity it wants: Lord Everard in... THE VAMPIRE ROSE: Everard, Tack, and Joden; all three possible introductions.
