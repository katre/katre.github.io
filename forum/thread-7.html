I'll try to pull together a map of Ussura this week and label it so you know what's where, but if you visit http://www.tlucretius.net/7thSea/Maps/Map_Ussura.htm you can see Pavtlow (where you are now), St. Tremult (a bit south of where the bulk of Montegue's navy currently is harbored), Odyesse (part of a northward spur of Somojez, and currently under control of Montegue's forces), and St. Andresgorod (capital of Somojez, and site of the sleeping drachen). Ideally, I'd like to find something you all can accomplish in not-too-many-more sessions, so we can call the season a wrap and take a break for a while. Feel free to discuss and/or ask questions about other unresolved plot threads you're interested in following up on.
